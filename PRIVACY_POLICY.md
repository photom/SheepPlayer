# Privacy Policy for SheepPlayer

*Last updated: August 12, 2025*

## Overview

SheepPlayer is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our music
player application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data

- **Music Files**: SheepPlayer accesses music files stored on your device to provide playback
  functionality
- **Playlists**: User-created playlists and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **App Settings**: User preferences and configuration settings

### Google Drive Integration

- **Authentication**: When you choose to connect Google Drive, we use OAuth 2.0 for secure
  authentication
- **File Access**: Access to music files stored in your Google Drive account (read-only access)
- **No File Storage**: We do not store your Google Drive files on our servers

## How We Use Your Information

- **Music Playback**: To play music files from your device or connected cloud storage
- **Personalization**: To remember your preferences, playlists, and settings
- **App Functionality**: To provide core music player features and maintain app state

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: All user data is stored locally on your device
- **No Data Transmission**: We do not transmit your personal data or music files to external servers
- **Secure Authentication**: Google Drive integration uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols

## Third-Party Services

### Google Drive API

- Used only when you explicitly choose to connect your Google Drive account
- Governed by Google's Privacy Policy
- We only request necessary permissions for music file access

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your music files and
personal data remain private and under your control.

## Your Rights

- **Data Control**: You have full control over your data stored locally on your device
- **Account Disconnection**: You can disconnect Google Drive integration at any time through app
  settings
- **Data Deletion**: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Children's Privacy

SheepPlayer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is
designed for general music playback and does not require personal information from users of any age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last
updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or SheepPlayer's privacy practices, please contact
us through the app's support channels.

---

*This privacy policy applies to SheepPlayer mobile application.*
